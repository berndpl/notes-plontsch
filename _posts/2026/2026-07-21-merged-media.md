There is something freeing about blurring the boundaries between text, sound, visuals, and material. It's both giving up and expanding on control for expression at the same time.

Here's a little experiment exploring merged media magazines with narration and ambience, which turns out to be especially appealing for foreign language content.

## Cloud × Local

Another interesting aspect is getting a feeling for using a LLM in the cloud for the image-based understanding in tandem with local models for narration and ambient sounds.
